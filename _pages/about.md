I am an MSc student in **Financial Risk and Data Analysis** at Sapienza University of Rome. My academic journey is driven by a deep interest in the intersection of finance, statistical modeling, and machine learning.

Currently, I am focusing on quantitative financial modeling and econometrics, with projects ranging from yield curve construction to volatility forecasting using neural networks. I am also collaborating on research involving Gaussian Process Regression.

I have a strong technical background in **Python**, **R**, and **C**, and I am experienced in working with high-performance computing environments. Beyond academia, I am passionate about free-diving and chess.
